FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ResumeForge really free?

Yes! ResumeForge is 100% free to use. Create, preview, and download your resume as PDF or Word without any charges. No hidden fees, ever.

Do I need to create an account?

No. ResumeForge works without any sign-up or login. Just open the builder and start creating your resume immediately.

Are the resumes ATS-friendly?

Yes. Our Modern Minimal template is specifically designed to pass Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) used by most companies for screening resumes.

Is my data safe and private?

Absolutely. All your data stays in your browser's local storage. We don't collect, store, or send any information to external servers.

Can I download my resume as a Word document?

Yes! You can download your resume as both PDF (print-ready) and Word (.docx) format so you can easily edit it later.

Can I use this on my phone?

Yes, ResumeForge is fully responsive and works on mobile phones, tablets, and desktops.

How do I save my progress?

Your resume is auto-saved in your browser. You can also export your data as a JSON file to back it up or transfer it to another device.
